Doing a transform on a grouped variable allows you, if you want, to use aggregate quantities for that group. For instance, the groups of males and females have different mean heights. Heres how you would calculate the difference of an individual from his or her groups mean.

NHANES <- group_by( NHANES, sex )
NHANES <- mutate( NHANES, dheight=height-mean(height) )
summarise( NHANES, sd(dheight) )
